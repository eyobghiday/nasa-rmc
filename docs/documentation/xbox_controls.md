# Manual Controls

## X - Box
### Driving
In the contorl center you select your driving mode
#### Arcade Drive
- **Left stick x:** turn
- **Left stick y:** throttle

#### Tank Drive
- **Right stick x:** turn
- **Left stick y;** throttle

### Trenchdigger
- A: tell `LinearActuatorPair` to go deeper
- B: tell `LinearActuatorPair` to go up
- X: toggle conveyor belt
